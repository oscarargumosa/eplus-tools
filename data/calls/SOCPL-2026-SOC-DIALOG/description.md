## Expected Outcome

Key expected results include:

(a) increased awareness of European social dialogue;

(b) enhanced European social dialogue at the sectoral and cross-industry levels;

(c) improved capacity of social partners, including in candidate countries.

These results should lead to a strengthened EU level social dialogue.

## Scope

This call aims at contributing to the promotion of social dialogue at cross-industry and sectoral level, developing European social dialogue, and building and strengthening the capacity of social partner organisations (both in Member States and candidate countries).
